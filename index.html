About Me

I am a second-year PhD student in the Ubiquitous Computing Lab at the University of Washington, advised by Vikram Iyer and Shwetak Patel. Before joining UW, I received my BS and MS in Computer Engineering at Northwestern University where I worked as an undergraduate research assistant for Josiah Hester and John A. Rogers. My research focuses on building embedded systems that demonstrate sensing, networking, and actuation despite tight power and form-factor constraints.
